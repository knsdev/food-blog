Recipe of the month

Spaghetti Bolognese

Everyone needs a great everyday Spaghetti Bolognese recipe, and this is mine! The Bolognese Sauce is rich, thick and has beautiful depth of flavour.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti (500g)
  • Minced meat (300 - 500g)
  • Oil
  • Onion (1x)
  • Sieved tomatoes (500g)
  • Tomato paste (100g)
  • Spices
    • Salt
    • Pepper
    • Oregano
    • Basil
    • Thyme
    • Paprika powder
  • Parmesan cheese

Instructions

  1. Boil some water in a cooking pot
  2. Add salt
  3. Add spaghetti
  4. Put some oil in your pan and heat it
  5. Add the minced meat
  6. Roast it for a few minutes
  7. Add the onion
  8. Lower the heat
  9. Add spices
  10. Add sieved tomatoes and tomato paste
  11. Mix it and cook the sauce for a few minutes
  12. Put the cooked spaghetti to the pan and mix everything together
  13. Sprinkle parmesan cheese on top and serve
  14. Bon appétit!
